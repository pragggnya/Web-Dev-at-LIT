Hardware

It is the physical components of a computer system, such as the central processing unit (CPU), memory, and storage devices.

Software

Software is the instructions that tell the hardware what to do. Software can be divided into two main categories: operating systems and applications.

Data

Data is a collection of numbers, characters, images or other outputs from devices that are used in computer systems.

algorithms

An algorithm is a list of rules to follow in order to solve a problem.

Computer Science

Abstraction

Abstraction is the process of taking away or removing characteristics from something in order to reduce it to a set of essential characteristics.

modularity

Modularity is the process of dividing a computer program into separate sub-programs.

Efficiency

Efficiency is the process of making something more efficient, such as a computer program or a machine.

Security

Security is the process of keeping data secure and safe from damage or theft.
